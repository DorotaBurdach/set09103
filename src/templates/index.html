What are Sacred Trees?




Sacred groves were used as place of worship in ancient times. They were a grouping of trees, chosen for their different qualities.
Sacred Trees were trees brought into the village, and were more accessible than the groves. There are many tales in history of important meetings between kings and tribal leaders taking place beneath sacred trees.


The wheel of the year is the celtic calendar, beginning and ending at Samhain. It is marked by the natural events in the year's cycle. Each tree has its own celtic name and its linked to the part of a year when its has the underlying energy.
